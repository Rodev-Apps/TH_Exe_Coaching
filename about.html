About
Tina Hanciles, Executive Coaching!

I am an entrepreneur with a background in Professional Development, an Educator, Clinical Practice Facilitator, with over 20 years nursing background.

My varied experience and expertise extends to helping the emerging, existing and transitioning leaders to operate effectively with boldness and courage by building trust with people in their organization. I have a strong passion to work alongside these leaders and support them in such a way that will enable them to identify their leadership footprint and thrive in their senior positions. Becoming the best means overcoming fear of failure or making mistakes which can often hold executives back from making bold decisions and taking necessary risks to take their teams forward.
